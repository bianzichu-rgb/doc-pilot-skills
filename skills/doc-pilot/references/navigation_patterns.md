## General Principles (Always Apply)

1. **Start simple** — the most common cause first, not the most dramatic
2. **Power cycle early** — reset/restart solves 40%+ of electronic faults
3. **Check connections before components** — loose plug before broken motor
4. **Back up before destructive steps** — firmware, factory reset, etc.
5. **Escalation trigger** — if 3+ steps fail, proactively suggest professional help
6. **Safety first** — unplug before any physical inspection step
